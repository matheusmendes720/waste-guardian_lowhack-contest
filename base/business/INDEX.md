## 📈 Industrial Intelligence Highlights

### Key Pain Points (F&B Industry)
1. **Setup failures** — Equipment misconfiguration causing waste
2. **Quality deviations** — Products failing quality control
3. **Downtime** — Unplanned stops losing production time
4. **Inventory** — Spoilage and obsolescence

### Benchmark Data
- Industry average waste: 3-7% of raw materials
- Typical cost impact: R$500K-2M/year per plant
- ESG reporting pressure: Increasing from investors
